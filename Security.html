Security














What we do


The security of your personal information and our website is our top priority – however you bank. And our concern for the safety of your financial and personal information goes beyond our banking relationship with you. We'll work with you to help prevent fraud, phishing, SMShing and vishing – and provide you with the latest tools and information to help safeguard your mobile, desktop and online experience. Our online and mobile security commitment FreeBank is committed to providing you with a secure online experience that protects our public website and Online Banking, our mobile apps and your confidential information. Our employees are trained on our security policies and procedures and work diligently to protect the integrity of your information. We use industry-accepted security practices, including firewalls and encryption, to safeguard the security of your personal financial information. These controls allow us to properly authenticate your identity when you access our online and mobile services and help to protect your information as it travels over the internet between your device and FreeBank. We also constantly monitor and assess the security of our website and mobile apps. With everything we're doing to protect your information, keep in mind that it is also important for you to take precautions to safeguard personal information on your end.

Explore more ways to protect your accounts

These simple steps can go a long way toward preventing online fraud


  • Never share your passwords and PINs, even with us
  • Change your passwords often
  • Don’t click on links you don't recognize
  • Never respond to texts or emails with personal info
  • Never respond to one time passcode requests that you didn't make
  • Don't use unknown links to access Online Banking
  • Don't deposit checks from unknown sources and transfer any portion of those funds
